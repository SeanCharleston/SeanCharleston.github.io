Technical Skills

PLC programming (Ladder Logic, Structured Text, Sequential Function Chart)

After some inital exposure to PLC programming at Stratex, I spent most of my first engineering work placement working with PLCs at Ballance Agri-Nutrients. The core of this work was to understand existing ladder logic from an obselete PLC, and produce updated code using ABB's PLC range. The work also included designing and interfacing to a new human machine interface (HMI), which required me to communicate directly with the workshop workers, to produce improved HMI features.

Computer programming (C, C++, C#, MATLAB, Simulink, LabVIEW)

Throughout my life I have been very familiar with computers and programming in general. I was first exposed to C during my time at Unitec, and continued to improve from there. During my time at the University of Waikato, many of my courses involved programming in C, C++, MATLAB, and simulink. This was continued in future jobs, specifically with embedded C programming and MATLAB. During my time at Plant & Food Research, I became familiar with LabView, and designed a software interface for acquiring data in real-time.

Time-of-Flight camera knowledge and experience

I have been working with time-of-flight cameras since my honours project in the final year of my Bachelor of Engineering degree.
